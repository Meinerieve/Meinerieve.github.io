Pallone Caravan

The Pallone Caravan is a Hub Area that you can enter from Mezeporta at HR17
If you talk to The Chief when you enter, you'll recieve a Caravan Gem

Pallone Carnival
・Great Reduction Festival：Slightly increases the chance of obtaining quest rewards, and also increases the chance of obtaining items in emergency missions.
・Great Bead Festival：Party points (PP) will increase 1.5 times more than normal
・Great Recommendation：Reputation points will be increased by 1.5 times than usual.
・Great Reward Festival：Greatly increases the chances of getting quest rewards

Facilities
・Hospitality Lounge　※You can enter with the reputation of 「Hard Working Hunter」
　Lounge Bento(1000CP)can be purchased.

・Guest House　※You can enter with the popularity 「Familiar Hunter」
　Inside is the Specialty Workshop, and Specialty Item Shop.
・Guest House Floor 2　※You can enter with the popularity 「Tale Telling Hunter」
Carnival Medicine：200000CP
　　Generate a Great ***** listed above randomly. It is effective when you are the owner of the quest (applies to all participants). 2 hours
