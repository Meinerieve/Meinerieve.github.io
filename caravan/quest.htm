Caravan Quest

Characteristics
・All materials obtained during the quest are converted into CP.
※Items obtained by poogie will be kept as material.
・There is an emergency mission during the quest, and if you complete it, you will get 500 CP or the following items.
　Common：Return Ball,Vitality Agent G,Sword Crystals,Monster Mats
　Grand Pallone Festival：Gunlance Coolant,Guts Tkt,Adrenaline Potion,Starving Wolf Potion,L.Sword Spirit Drug
　Non Grand Pallone Festival：PoisonDefenceTkt,Sleep Defence Tkt,Para Defence Tkt,Emergency Bag
・There is no disadvantage in clearing via a sub-target.
・The quests are the same as those of the Normal Hunting.
・The quests you can post are limited by the * rank you've cleared.Participation is open to everyone regardless of *rank。
・If you return halfway through, you will receive the number of recognition, CP, etc. that you have earned so far.
・Only ★0 will not get the hunter recognition.
Tension gauge (verification required)
・By filling up the Tension Gauge, your attack and defense power will increase. Accumulate the gauge to the maximum and get 1.5 times atk.
・クエDuring a strike, attacking an ally raises the tension gauge of the attacked side.
・The amount of increase in the gauge is determined according to the Route difference. 1 hit increases attack and defense power by 1.01 times (up to half of the gauge).
・Also, tension may increase depending on the route difference cleared at the start of the quest (up to half of the gauge)
・Tension UP at the start of the quest + Tension UP during the quest will maximize the gauge.
