My Tore Bar

The number of cats coming to My Tore increases.
You can hear information such as skills related to cat, open-air baths, and personalities of cats in small talk.

A "hospitality" section is added at Lv2.
If "Hospitality Settings" is ON, Other Players will receive an item 10 times a day when they select "Hospitality" at the bar.
The items you will receive are those on the guild special request list of the bar owner.

・Boiled Golden Egg
・Boiled Silver Egg
・Hot & Cold Drink
・Herbal Medicine G
・Disposable Earplugs
・HGE Earplugs
・Hi-Speed Whetstone

・Anti-Flinch Fruit

At Lv3, you can get "Regulars' Gifts" 3 times a day when you select "Talk" at your own bar.

You can ask a hunter to introduce you to a Felyne that he or she has hired, and you can hire him or her for your own My Tore
