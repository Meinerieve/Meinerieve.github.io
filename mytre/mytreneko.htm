Attributes(MAX255)
It has a Affection (affects the items brought back in the adventure) and cattiness (sales of the store).
The two Attributes increase differently depending on the type of shop.

ShopHow it affects lvls
My Tore CombineIncreased cattiness (rarely decreases affection)
My Tore General StoreIncreased cattiness (rarely decreases affection)
My Tore BarRaises an average amount
My Tore ClothingCattiness increases slightly(rarely decreases affection)

The type of cat depends on the number of shops, and the number of cats that come to the square at one time depends on the level of the bar.
