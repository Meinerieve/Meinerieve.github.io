○Basic Info
・Grand tournament (pvp) is only available at night
・Defeat the opponent's monster in 1v1 or 2v2
・Must complete up to the quest "Usurping the Queen!" in Hunter Dojo
・If you run out of power during the process, you cannot move for 10 seconds.
・Items used during the grand tournament will be returned after the event is over.

○Monster Status
・Health changes depending on fighting skill LV (strength + condition)
・Friendship level affects the increase of the wrath gauge when instructions by flutes are successful.

○What is the wrath gauge?
・The length of the attack time with the wrath flute. Base is 15 seconds and increases up to 105 seconds
