Gooks

You can keep up to 5 gooks in My Garden. [HR5-]
When a hunter reaches the G rank, "Gooks" are promoted to "G level Gooks".

Raising Gooks
Talk to the caretaker cat in My Garden and receive the "Seek Eggs" request.
Then talk to the guild master and complete one of the "Seek Eggs" quests!

Seek Eggs [Jungle Kut-Ku]White
Seek Eggs [Swamp Congalala]Yellow
Seek Eggs [Desert Daimyo]Black
Seek Eggs [Volcano Gravios](HR11～)Brown
Seek Eggs [Snowy Mtn Tigrex](HR51～)Blue

After clearing the "Seek Eggs," the hunter speaks to the caretaker cat again and receives another quest to search for tickets.
After collecting the tickets talk to the caretaker cat and complete any quest once, and afterwards an egg will appear.
Clear 4 more quests and a Gook will hatch!
(During this time, it is possible to sing to them, etc.)
After the gook is born, talk to the caretaker cat to receive the Playground Ticket Search quest.

Caring for Gooks
You can receive a batch of items from the housekeeper.
They may also give you new eggs, which may be a different color than the ones you obtained from the quest.
You can also exchange items, redecorate, and add clothing to gooks.
