My Garden

By talking to the straw cat, you can request "watering," "cleaning," "cooking," "mining," "catching," and "Rubbish".
On rare occasions you can select "bargains".
You may only request an item one time per quest. After the next successful or failed hunt you can select again.
The "cooking" and "rubbish" options require you to have the requisite items in your inventory
Items in "rubbish" vary according to the number of quests completed. Clearing via sub-quest counts towards this amount.
You will sometimes receive various items talking to the garden cats.
A lost and found cat can sometimes appear and ask you to find a specific item to bring to it.

Garden Rank
You will receive a stamp when you purchase furniture with materials from the general store "Farm and Furniture" section.
One stamp will be added for each furniture purchase that uses at least one material and costs 3000z in a single purchase.
(With EX course enabled it will be 1 stamp for every 2000z used.)
By collecting these stamps, you can rank up the "Lucky tools" and "Super tools" that you use in My Garden.
