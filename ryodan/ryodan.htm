Guilds

Create/Join a Guild
・Guilds can be created at HR5 or higher
・You cannot create a guild with the same name as another.
・Creation of a guild and application for membership are carried out by the guild receptionist in Mezeporta.

Maintaining a Guild
・There are 4 or more characters of Hunter Rank (HR) 3 or higher who have played MHF within the past month in the guild.
・Inactive guilds will be disbanded, checked at the time of maintanence each Wednesday.
・If the conditions are not met at that point, the guild association will send mail to the guild leader.
1st: Attention mail
2nd: Warning mail
3rd: Forced disband mail
・The third time, the group is forcibly disbanded and a disbandment mail is sent to all guild members.

Changing Guild Leaders
If the Guild Leader does not log in for 2 consecutive weeks, the second in command will become the new Guild Leader.
The third player in the guild list will then be made the new second in command.

Restrictions on disbanding or leaving a guild
Guild leader (disbanded): Cannot create or apply to join a guild for 5 days (120 hours) after disbandment.
Guild member (kicked): Cannot create a guild or apply for membership for 3 days (72 hours) after being kicked out.
Guild members (leave): cannot create a guild or apply for membership for 10 days (240 hours) after leaving a guild.

Alliance
Alliances can be joined by up to three guilds.
Once an alliance is formed, the use of alliance chat is enabled.
Registration for the Hunters' Festival will be on an alliance basis and all guilds will be on the same team.

Guild Rank
Bonuses for increasing hunting party ranks will be available after each scheduled maintenance.
Hunting points (RP) will accumulate 1P after logging in for a total of 30 minutes. (15 minutes/1P at authorized NetCafes)
Other RPs can be obtained through official hunting competitions.
