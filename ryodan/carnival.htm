Hunter Festival

The Hunters' Festival is a festival in which guilds in each server are divided into Red Dragon and Blue Dragon groups to collect souls and compete for the number of souls collected.
The Hunters' Festival will be held in the following cycle

１：Registration Festival, The leader of the guild registers to participate in the Hunters' Festival (from guild rank 1).
　　　　　　　At that time, the leader can vote for one trial that can be occupied.
２：Soul Festival,　The souls are delivered by accomplishing the trials designated for each of its hunter festivals.
　　　　　　　The souls you acquire will be automatically entered by talking to the "Hunter's Festival Receptionist" in Mezeporta Square.
　　　　　　　(Note that you can only carry a maximum of 500 souls.)
３：Tally Festival, Operational
４：Prize Festival,　Depending on the winners and losers, you will receive different armor, festival points, etc.

Secret Quests（bonus trials）
A bonus that occurs to the losing group three times per day (4:00, 12:00, 20:00).
Which quest you get a bonus for is different for each hunter festival.
Secret quests occur only for 2 hours from 6:00 to 9:00, 14:00 to 18:00, and 22:00 to 1:00.

Trial Occupancy
During the following period, one monster may be occupied by the opposing side, and no souls may be received for hunting.
　　You can check which monsters are occupied at the "Hunters' Festival Reception".
　Fri/Sat: 12:00-18:00, Sun/Mon: 18:00-24:00

Rewards
・Armor　All of them become degraded equipment after the reward festival.
　Winners: After the festival [winning cats] Losers: After the festival [losers].

・Festival Points
Festival points can have a maximum of 60,000P, and are calculated using the following formula. (only leader is doubled)
((total number of souls of members + result of opposing team) ÷ number of souls) x Achievement bonus + win/loss points
