○Fundamentals
・Raising the display level (★1-4) increases the variety of furniture and makes it easier to get points.
・Stamps required for display level up and ticket purchases accumulate 1 stamp for every 3000z of furniture "purchased materials".
・★1 = 30 stamps, ★2 = 30 stamps, ★3 = 50 stamps, ★4 = 50 stamps. In other words, if ★4 is used, 160 stamps = 480,000 z
・Tournament and judgment tickets are purchased after arranging the furniture ※Note
・Canceling a tournament does not return tickets, furniture remains
・Placement of furniture does not affect points, but pay attention to placement luck
・The number of pieces of furniture that can be placed is limited to 50. The number of pieces placed can be checked on the store's purchase screen.

○Points
・10 of the same color will get a bonus of 200p per piece.
・10 pieces of the same attribute will get a bonus of 200p per piece.
・Minus points for 10 or more pieces of the same furniture
・Only the tableware can be the same, so combining them will increase the score
(Tableware has different points depending on its color. White has the highest score)
・In some cases, the score is higher for different colors and sizes, e.g., 9:1.
・Placement
