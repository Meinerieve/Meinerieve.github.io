Rasta Enhancement

・The same settings as yours are used for appearance. (Cannot be changed)
・Weapons and armor can be equipped with items that you own.
・It is possible for you and Rasta to be equipped with the same weapons and armor.
※However, items equipped by Rasta cannot be upgraded or sold.

Unlocking Weapon Types
・Unlock Procedure
　1.Select "Release rasta function" → "Unlock weapon type" → "Hunting order required for release"
　2.Select the target monster from the "Hunting order list" and accept the order. (only one at a time)
　3.Hunt the target monster, regardless of whether it is a high or low rank monster.
　4.Select a weapon from "Release rasta function" → "Unlock weapon type" → "Select weapon type to release"
　　※The number of target monsters cleared is the number of weapon types that can be opened. Any of them can be opened in any order.

Weapon LV and Armor LV release
・Release Procedure
　1.Select "Release Rasta Functions" and then "Release Armor LV."
　　Guild loyalty UP: By releasing it, you can draw out attack power and attribute attack power
　　Guild reliability UP: By releasing it, you can draw out defense power and the number of skill activations
　2.Pay guild contribution points according to open LV
