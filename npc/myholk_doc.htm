Halk

They will go on quests with you and help you defeat monsters.[HR1～]
Unlike Rasta, it is not included in the number of participants, and 4 halks will participate in a 4-person PT.
By raising them, you can increase their strength, attack power, etc., and also enable them to attack with elements.
When completing hunting quests, halk feathers, halk calls, and books of learning may be dropped.

My Halk
You can call the halk when you approach the rock in My Support.
When the Halk is on the rock, you can feed it.
By talking to the instructor, you can set up the book of learning skills and change the name.

Status
The initial value is randomly distributed so that the total of health, attack power, defense, and intellect is 10.
"Intimacy"、"Health"、"Attack"、"Defense"、"Intellect" Halk will level up when all five values reach the right end of the gauge.
LV1:Max 255　LV2:Max 510　LV3:Max 765
As they level up, not only do their abilities change but also the halk's appearance changes.
