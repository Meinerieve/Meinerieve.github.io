Partner Skills

Guild contribution points are spent to learn Skills.
Skills have a "cost", and there are restrictions on activating them.
Default cost 50, 5 skill slots
You gain +1 skill slots at PR50, 300, 500, 700, and 900
Cost is +10 for every 50 PR
